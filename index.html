ABOUT ME

A Web and Mobile Computing Student at Rochester Institute of Technology and a New-York-City resident who enjoys travelling, I favor and specialize in Front-End Web Development, and have a hands-on approach to both designing and building mobile apps for the iOS and Android platforms. Since middle school, I have delved into many fascinating websites and as I began exploring more, I have gained further interest in how they are developed, and how their features bring great User Experience (UX) to the digital world. I frequently use the popular sites — Google, Facebook, and LinkedIn — and highly enjoy their common feature: an ease of communication, and accessibility. I'm also inspired by an eclectic of resourceful video/tutorial sites: Youtube, Lynda.com, and Treehouse; they simplify the way I normally read texts by displaying captions for me to follow along with the audio. Importantly, these tutorial sites offer exercise files for learners like me to view, and follow along with the instructors in the different videos. Due to my curiosity of these dynamic and interactive websites, and my continuous interest in responsive web development, I look forward to become a future Front-End Developer, who will make interactive websites more mobile-friendly, with my progressing knowledge of both UX principles, and development tools.
